## в—Ҳ About

**Beckoned** is a first-person psychological horror game inspired by *PT* and *Silent Hill*, built in **Godot Engine 4**.

A man accepts an invitation from a mysterious woman to her home. As she leads him through the corridors вҖ” seductive, elusive, always just ahead вҖ” the house begins to *change*. Every room he leaves becomes something else. The further he goes, the more distorted reality becomes, until the occult geometry of the house reveals its true purpose.

> *She was beautiful. The house was not.*

---

## в—Ҳ Core Mechanic

The central mechanic of **Beckoned** is **room mutation**:

- Every time the player enters a new room, the **previous room transforms**
- Rooms become increasingly bizarre, hostile, and satanic in atmosphere
- There is no going back вҖ” only forward, deeper into the labyrinth
- The house is alive. It remembers where you've been.

---

## в—Ҳ Lore

The woman is not human.

She appears at the beginning вҖ” elegant, warm, irresistible. She draws the protagonist through her home room by room, always retreating, always beckoning. As the architecture warps around him, her true nature surfaces: a **demonic entity** using seduction as a ritual, and the protagonist as its offering.

**The woman has three forms:**

| Phase | Description | Trigger |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **The Seductress** | Fully human. Beautiful. Distant. | Rooms 1вҖ“3 |
| **The Distortion** | Wrong proportions. Impossible movements. | Rooms 4вҖ“7 |
| **The True Form** | Demonic. Winged. The ritual complete. | Final room |
